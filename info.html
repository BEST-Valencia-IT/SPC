Info about the course:

The course will host 22 participants from all around Europe . Participants will only have to pay a reduced fee. This way, all the food, accomodation and transportation expenses during the course will be covered. Weekend Trip and City Rally are included in the fee.
Those students who pass the course will receive a diploma and some free-elective credits, although these credits are still not confirmed.
The Course Syllabus as well as some pre-materials will be sent to the participants via email before the course.

For further information please check the Survival Guide or contact us.
