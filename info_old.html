Info about the course:

The course will host 24 participants, 22 european and 2 local, promoting the Europeanisation of our university.
The course will mainly consist about how to create and develop Start Ups .
These will be mandatory lessons taught in English by specialized UPV teachers. You will only have to pay a non-significant part of the course's cost. This way all food and host expenses during the course will be covered, as well as the Weekend Trip, and city Rally.
If they pass it, they will receive a diploma and maybe even some free-elective credits, though it is still no confirmed.
Course syllabus and pre-materials will be sent to the participants via email before the course.
More information regarding the SpC16 Valencia can be found in the Survival Guide.
